Default player controls:
Player 1:
    Movement - Arrow keys
    Shooting - Enter
    Stop - right Ctrl
Player 2:
    Movement - WASD keys
    Shooting - Shift
    Stop - left Ctrl

Global controls:
Pause: P
Help: H
Restart: R
Exit: Escape

The objective of the game is to destroy your opponent.
Avoid mines and obstacles on the map.
